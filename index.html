Anidra

Anidra or Insomnia is the most commonly reported sleep problem, which profoundly affects the person’s quality of life.

Cervical

Cervical spondylosis is the medical term for neck pain caused by age-related changes to bones and tissues.

Knee Pain

The most common causes of knee pain are related to aging, injury or repeated stress on the knee.

Hemicrania

Hemicrania continua is a chronic and persistent form of headache and always occurs on the same side of the face and head.

Sciatica

Sciatica happens when inflammation, pinching or compression affect one or more nerves that run down your lower back and into your legs

Paralysis

If you have paralysis, you are partly or entirely unable to move the affected parts of the body.

Frozen Shoulder

Also known as adhesive capsulitis, frozen shoulder is usually caused by inflammation.

Slip Disk

A slipped disc is when a soft cushion of tissue between the bones in your spine pushes out. It's painful if it presses on nerves.
